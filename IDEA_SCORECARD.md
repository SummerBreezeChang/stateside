## Stress test for the winner

- What is the riskiest assumption?
- What can be proven in the first 60–90 minutes?
- What is the smallest testable public demo?
- What external dependency could fail, and what is the fallback?
- Why is GPT-5.6 the right engine for the core interaction?
- What part will Codex help build, test, debug, or evaluate unusually quickly?
